About Zerodha

Zerodha is India’s largest stock broker offering retail and institutional broking, investment, and trading services. We are known for transparent pricing, powerful platforms, and a focus on customer-first principles.

Founded in 2010, Zerodha has disrupted the brokerage industry by introducing low-cost, technology-driven trading solutions. Today, we serve millions of investors and traders across the country.
